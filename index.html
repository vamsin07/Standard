Machine Learning Projects

Image Reconstruction using FA-VAE

Developed a novel Frequency Augmented Variational Autoencoder (FA-VAE) architecture to address quality degradation in image reconstruction tasks. The model utilized Frequency Complement Modules (FCMs) to process high and low-frequency components of images separately and implemented a Dynamic Spectrum Loss (DSL) mechanism to enhance feature extraction. This approach improved image reconstruction accuracy by 25% compared to baseline models and demonstrated significant robustness in handling noisy datasets.
